Timeboxed watchface

Pebble Health
Enables showing Pebble Health data. We show steps and distance by default, but you can enable showing sleep and deep sleep times after you wake up.
If set, the watch will show the sleep and deep sleep time for that night for an hour after you wake up, switching back to steps after that time.
Weather
Enables showing weather data. This requires a WeatherUnderground API key. Go to wunderground.com to create a free account and get a key.

Colors
